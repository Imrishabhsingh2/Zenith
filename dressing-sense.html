Core Principles

I. The Law of Proportions

Your tie width must match your lapel width. Your jacket should just cover your seat. The break on your trousers should be minimal or non-existent. Fit dictates authority.

II. Leather Harmony

Your shoes must match your belt. Your watch strap should harmonize with both. Brown with brown, black with black. Never mix formal leathers.
